One of our e-commerce clients generates $200K per month from automated email flows alone. No sends from the team. No promotions. Just five sequences running in the background.

The five flows: welcome sequence (10-15% of email revenue), abandoned cart (25-35%), post-purchase (15-20%), win-back (5-10%), and browse abandonment (10-15%).

If your store does $500K/month, well-built email flows should generate $50-100K of that. If email is less than 15% of your revenue, you are leaving serious money on the table.

The technology has gotten absurdly good. Klaviyo, Customer.io, and others make it possible to build sophisticated automation without engineering resources. The bottleneck is not the tool. It is the strategy behind the flows, the copy, the timing, and the segmentation. Most brands are still sending the same generic welcome email they set up three years ago.
