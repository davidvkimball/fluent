A one-second delay in page load time reduces conversions by 7%. That is not opinion. It is data from Google, Amazon, and Walmart repeated across hundreds of studies.

Most agency websites load in 4-8 seconds. Most client websites are even worse. Every one of those seconds is revenue disappearing.

The usual suspects are always the same: bloated frameworks, unoptimized images, and third-party scripts nobody audits. A simple marketing site does not need React. A 3MB hero image served at full resolution to a mobile phone is malpractice. And that chat widget adding 500ms? Probably not worth it.

We build on Astro, which renders to static HTML by default. Zero JavaScript unless explicitly needed. Combined with image optimization and inlined CSS, our sites consistently score 95+ on Lighthouse. Speed is not a feature. It is the foundation.
